Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy covers how LessLens handles your personal information and data.

Information gathering and usage

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in the LessLens app. Individual settings relating to the LessLens app are not personal and are stored only on your device.

Photos, Scans, and Images

We use the functionality of your device’s TrueDepth sensor to take a scan of your face and derive the specific measurements to optimally display a model of glasses or sunglasses on your face. When you submit (or grant us permission to take) a photo, facial scan, or other image of you, we don’t share those photos, scans, images, or any facial data captured in the facial scans, with any third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 (“Children”). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us. If we discover that a Child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Deleting Your Data

You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us by email.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about data privacy, you can also contact us by email at support@lesslens.com.
